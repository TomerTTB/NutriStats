Food Diary Documentation

Welcome to the Food Diary documentation! This guide will help you understand how to use the application effectively to track your nutrition and weight goals.

What is Food Diary?

Food Diary is a comprehensive nutrition and weight tracking application that helps you:

  • Track your daily food intake and nutrition
  • Monitor your weight progress
  • Generate detailed nutrition reports
  • Manage your own food database

Key Features

Food Diary

Record your daily meals and track your nutrition intake. The diary provides a detailed breakdown of calories, proteins, carbs, and fats for each meal.

Weight Tracking

Monitor your weight progress with detailed statistics including:

  • Today's Weight - Your most recent weight measurement
  • Last Change - The difference from your previous weight entry
  • Overall Change - Total weight change since you started tracking
  • Average Change - The average change between consecutive measurements
  • Progress - Your current weight trend

Reports

Get detailed insights into your nutrition and weight data through comprehensive reports and charts.

Foods Database

Access and manage a customizable database of foods with their nutritional information.

Security & Authentication

Your data is protected with enterprise-level security features:

  • JWT-based stateless authentication
  • Bcrypt password hashing with salt rounds
  • Rate limiting for login attempts
  • Input validation and sanitization
  • Protected API endpoints
  • Secure token management
