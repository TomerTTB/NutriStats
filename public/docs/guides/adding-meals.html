Adding Meals to Your Food Diary

This guide will walk you through the process of adding meals to your food diary, helping you track your daily nutrition intake effectively.

Getting to the Food Diary

To start adding meals, navigate to the Food Diary page by clicking on "Diary" in the main navigation menu.

Adding a New Meal

Step 1: Select the Date

Use the date picker at the top of the diary page to select the date for which you want to add a meal. By default, today's date is selected.

Step 2: Choose Meal Type

Click on the appropriate meal section:

  • Breakfast - Morning meals
  • Lunch - Midday meals
  • Dinner - Evening meals
  • Snacks - Any snacks throughout the day

Step 3: Add Food Items

Within each meal section, you can add individual food items:

  1. Click the "Add Food" button in the desired meal section
  2. Search for the food item using the search box
  3. Select the food from the dropdown suggestions
  4. Enter the quantity/serving size
  5. Click "Add" to include it in your meal

Food Search Tips

  • Be specific: Search for "chicken breast grilled" rather than just "chicken"
  • Use common names: Try both brand names and generic terms
  • Check units: Pay attention to serving sizes (grams, cups, pieces, etc.)
  • Create custom foods: If you can't find a food, you can add it to your personal database

Editing Meal Entries

To modify an existing meal entry:

  1. Navigate to the date with the meal you want to edit
  2. Find the food item in the appropriate meal section
  3. Click the edit icon next to the food item
  4. Adjust the quantity or replace the food item
  5. Save your changes

Deleting Meal Entries

To remove a food item from a meal:

  1. Locate the food item you want to remove
  2. Click the delete/trash icon next to the item
  3. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Copying Meals

Save time by copying meals from previous days:

  1. Navigate to a day with meals you want to copy
  2. Click the "Copy Meal" button for the specific meal section
  3. Select the target date where you want to paste the meal
  4. Confirm the copy operation

Viewing Nutrition Summary

As you add meals, the nutrition summary at the bottom of the page will automatically update to show:

  • Total calories for the day
  • Macronutrient breakdown (proteins, carbs, fats)
  • Progress toward your daily nutrition goals

Best Practices

  • Log meals promptly: Add meals shortly after eating for better accuracy
  • Be accurate with portions: Use measuring tools when possible
  • Include everything: Don't forget cooking oils, condiments, and beverages
  • Review regularly: Check your entries for completeness and accuracy

Troubleshooting

Can't find a food item?

If you can't find a specific food in the database:

  • Try different search terms or spellings
  • Look for similar foods and adjust the quantity
  • Add the food to your personal database via the Foods page

Wrong serving size?

If the default serving size doesn't match what you ate:

  • Adjust the quantity field (e.g., 0.5 for half a serving, 2 for double)
  • Convert between units if needed (100g = 3.5oz approximately)
