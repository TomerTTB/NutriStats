Food Diary

The Food Diary is the core feature of the application, allowing you to track your daily meals and monitor your nutrition intake in real-time.

Diary Overview

Daily Structure

Your food diary is organized by:

  • Days: Each day has its own diary page
  • Meal Times: Customizable time slots for your meals
  • Food Items: Individual foods and portions for each meal

Key Features

Meal Management

  • Flexible Meal Times:
    • Adjust meal times to match your schedule
    • Add or remove meal slots as needed
    • Automatically spaced based on your meal interval setting
  • Food Entry:
    • Quick food search with auto-complete
    • Portion size adjustment
    • Multiple items per meal

Nutrition Tracking

  • Real-time Totals:
    • Daily calorie count
    • Macronutrient breakdown (protein, carbs, fat)
    • Progress towards daily goals
  • Meal-specific Totals:
    • Calories per meal
    • Macronutrients per meal
    • Percentage of daily targets

Using the Food Diary

Navigation

  • Day Selection:
    • Use the calendar to select different days
    • Quick navigation between days
    • Today's diary is loaded by default
  • Meal Navigation:
    • Scroll through meals chronologically
    • Click any meal to expand/collapse details
    • Quick jump to specific meal times

Adding Foods

  1. Click on a meal time slot
  2. Use the search bar to find foods
  3. Select the food from search results
  4. Adjust the portion size
  5. Click "Add" to include in your meal

Editing and Deleting

  • Edit Food Items:
    • Click the edit icon next to any food
    • Adjust portion size or details
    • Save changes
  • Remove Items:
    • Click the delete icon
    • Confirm deletion
    • Totals update automatically

Tips and Best Practices

  • Log foods as you eat them for better accuracy
  • Use the quick search feature for frequently eaten foods
  • Check your daily totals against your goals
  • Adjust portion sizes to match your actual intake
  • Review your diary at the end of each day
