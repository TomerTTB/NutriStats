Reports

The Reports section provides comprehensive insights into your nutrition and weight tracking data, helping you understand your progress and make informed decisions about your health goals.

Report Types

Nutrition Report

  • Weekly Calorie Achievement:
    • Visual chart showing daily calorie goals vs. actual intake
    • Week-by-week comparison
    • Progress indicators
  • Macro Breakdown:
    • Daily protein, carbs, and fat intake
    • Percentage of targets achieved
    • Weekly averages

Weight Tracking

  • Weight Statistics:
    • Today's Weight - Current weight measurement
    • Last Change - Most recent weight change
    • Overall Change - Total change since starting
    • Average Change - Typical change between measurements
    • Progress - Overall trend indicator
  • Weight Chart:
    • Visual representation of weight changes
    • Trend line for progress visualization
    • Interactive data points

Understanding Your Reports

Nutrition Analysis

  • Calorie Goals:
    • Daily target vs. actual intake
    • Weekly average calculations
    • Deficit/surplus indicators
  • Macronutrient Balance:
    • Protein intake relative to body weight
    • Fat intake analysis
    • Carbohydrate distribution

Weight Progress

  • Trend Analysis:
    • Short-term changes
    • Long-term progress
    • Rate of change indicators
  • Progress Indicators:
    • Direction arrows
    • Rate of change
    • Goal progress

Using the Reports

Navigation

  • Report Selection:
    • Switch between nutrition and weight reports
    • Select different time periods
    • Filter data as needed
  • Interactive Elements:
    • Click chart points for details
    • Hover over bars for specific values
    • Use legends to toggle data series

Data Management

  • Weight Entries:
    • Add new weight measurements
    • Edit existing entries
    • Remove incorrect data
  • Data Export:
    • Download reports
    • Share progress
    • Back up data

Tips for Reading Reports

  • Focus on trends rather than daily fluctuations
  • Compare weekly averages for more accurate progress tracking
  • Use the interactive features to explore your data
  • Check your progress against your personal goals
  • Look for patterns in your nutrition and weight data
