Account Setup

Setting up your Food Diary account properly is crucial for accurate tracking and personalized recommendations. This guide will walk you through the complete setup process.

Creating Your Account

Registration Process

  1. Navigate to the login page
  2. Click the "Register" button
  3. Fill in your details:
    • Email address (will be your login username)
    • Password (minimum 8 characters)
    • Name (how you'll be addressed in the app)
  4. Click "Register" to create your account

Initial Settings Configuration

Personal Information

Navigate to the Settings page to configure your personal details:

  • Unit System: Choose between metric (kg/cm) or imperial (lbs/inches)
  • Sex: Select your biological sex for metabolic calculations
  • Age: Enter your current age
  • Height: Your height in the selected unit system
  • Weight: Your current weight (will be your first weight entry)

Activity Level Settings

Select your activity level from the following options:

  • 1.2: Sedentary (little or no exercise)
  • 1.375: Lightly active (light exercise 1-3 days/week)
  • 1.55: Moderately active (moderate exercise 3-5 days/week)
  • 1.725: Very active (hard exercise 6-7 days/week)
  • 1.9: Extra active (very hard exercise & physical job)

Nutrition Goals

Configure your nutrition targets:

  • Calorie Adjustment: Fine-tune your daily calorie target
  • Meal Interval: Set preferred hours between meals (1-6 hours)
  • Protein Level: Set your protein intake goal (g/kg of body weight)
  • Fat Level: Set your fat intake goal (g/kg of body weight)

Customizing Your Experience

Meal Times

The app automatically creates default meal times based on your meal interval setting. You can:

  • Adjust meal times in the Food Diary page
  • Add or remove meal times as needed
  • Set custom names for your meals

Food Database

Before starting to track meals:

  • Browse the existing foods database
  • Add any common foods you frequently eat
  • Customize portion sizes for your needs
