Quick Start Guide

Get started with Food Diary in just a few simple steps. This guide will walk you through the basics of setting up your account and recording your first meal.

1. Create Your Account

  1. Click the "Login" button in the top navigation bar
  2. Select "Register" to create a new account
  3. Fill in your email, password, and name
  4. Click "Register" to create your account

2. Set Up Your Profile

  1. Go to the Settings page
  2. Enter your basic information:
    • Height
    • Weight
    • Age
    • Activity Level
  3. Save your settings

3. Record Your First Meal

  1. Navigate to the Food Diary page
  2. Click on a meal time slot
  3. Use the food search to find your food
  4. Enter the amount consumed
  5. Click "Add" to record the food

4. Track Your Weight

  1. Go to the Reports page
  2. Click on the "Weight Tracking" tab
  3. Enter your current weight
  4. Click "Save" to record your weight
